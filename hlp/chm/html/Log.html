The activity log

MattPlot records meticulously all his activity in a log; when something goes wrong it may be a good idea inspect its lines.

The log window can be opened by  ShowLog action or clicking on the statusbar; remember that you can use mouse to drag and dock the log in the place you want, and to select and copy its content.

The log can also be useful during Measure mode to collect point coordinates.
